CARNIVOROUS ANIMALS

Carnivorous animals, often referred to as carnivores, are those that primarily feed on the flesh of other animals. These predators play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of ecosystems by controlling the populations of other species. Carnivores can be found across various habitats and exhibit a range of adaptations that make them efficient hunters.
