Omnivorous animals, or omnivores, are those that have the ability to consume and digest both plant and animal matter. This diverse diet allows them to adapt to a wide range of environments and food sources. Omnivores play important roles in ecosystems by controlling populations of both plants and animals and helping to disperse seeds.
